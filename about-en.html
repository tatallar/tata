About Ives Imports

Ives Imports was born in 2016 as a possibility for medium size and small companies, to offer them the access to International Markets. We are an External Purchasing Department, being in charge of the full management of International purchases for your Company.

Our idea arises from the difficulties of some small and medium companies to hire specialized workers in International Trade. We DO believe our working model is a great advantage, as far as meeting the targets is concerned, minimizing the costs of having internal structure of workers.

Our profesional network with more tan 15 years of experience in different sectors, works in an individualised way for your company, as an extension of it, offering continuous follow up to cover our customers needs. We like to feel a part of your company, with a continuous follow-up, working partially in our customers properties, this is subject to be agreed according to the volume and needs.

We carry out the market prospection, regarding the search of products and suppliers worldwide. We visit the different trade shows to find the suitable products and suppliers, and to be updated with the last tendencies of your sector.


Our tasks are:

  • To manage and plan the International Purchases of your company.

  • Implant the purchasing policy, following the guidelines given by the company.

  • Search and selection of products and suppliers in an active way, in the international framework. Continuous prospection of the market according to our customersâ€™ needs.

  • We assist to tradeshows, and program commercial travel to visit the different suppliers, follow up production and quality inspections.

  • Samples management and development, agreeing with the company the optimum results, regarding prices, quality and volume.

  • Integral Import Logistics, including the different stages, customs clearance and final delivery of the goods to our customers.

  • Stock control and planning of purchases according to the needs and following the purchase budget, that is decided together with the management team.

  • Support for stock control, movement of goods, and optimum performance of ERP System.

  • Collaboration with other departments, like comercial or financial, to achieve the objectives.
